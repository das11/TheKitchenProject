About Cyber Trivia

Cyber Trivia , as the name stands, is a trivia game on Cyber related topics.
 This app aims to spread Cyber awareness amongs the childern, to teach them how to carefully and skillfully use
 the internet so that they may not fall victim to the hands of hackers and cyber crime activities. 
The player will be given some questions on how he/she should act when he/she is using the internet . 
There are three levels of questions.
 In each level, the player will be given 3 lives.
The player should give the correct answers to the questions.
In case the player gives the wrong answer, he/she will be deducted a life.
The app also provides the reason to why each option is correct or wrong, thus, educating the player.
